Tuna Fish Salad

Description:

Tuna fish salad is a popular cold salad that is made using canned tuna as the main ingredient. The tuna is typically mixed with mayonnaise, chopped celery, onion, and pickles, along with a variety of seasonings such as salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Some variations of the salad may also include chopped hard-boiled eggs, mustard, or lemon juice for added flavor. The mixture is then chilled in the refrigerator for a period of time to allow the flavors to meld together. Tuna fish salad is commonly served on a bed of lettuce, as a sandwich filling, or as a topping for crackers. It is a quick and easy meal that is perfect for lunch, dinner, or as a snack.

Ingridents:

  • 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup chopped celery
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • ½ teaspoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch paprika, or to taste

Steps:

  1. Combine tuna, mayonnaise, celery, onion, parsley, lemon juice, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a large bowl; mix well. Season with paprika; refrigerate until chilled.
